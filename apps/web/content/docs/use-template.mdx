---
title: How to Use a Template
description: A practical workflow for selecting, evaluating, and adopting templates safely.
---

## 1) Choose the right starting point

Use the discovery pages to narrow options:

- Category pages for use-case fit
- Latest for recent releases
- Popular for proven demand

Then review description, version notes, and seller status.

## 2) Evaluate trust signals before use

- Seller verification status
- Release/update recency
- Listing clarity and quality

## 3) Test in a controlled environment first

Run templates in a non-production environment before connecting real systems or sensitive data.

Validate:

- Expected outputs
- Tool behavior
- Failure handling

## 4) Customize to your workflow

Adjust prompts, tools, and runtime settings once baseline behavior is confirmed.

## 5) Adopt and keep updated

Track updates and review "what's new" notes to stay aligned with fixes and improvements.

## Safety reminder

Templates are accelerators, not blind trust artifacts. Keep review and testing in your loop.

Read [Why it is safe](/docs/safety) for platform safeguards and limits.
If you are comparing seller incentives, read [Sell Templates + Pricing](/docs/sell-templates-pricing).
