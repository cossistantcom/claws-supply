To sell paid templates, complete the verification flow from your profile settings.

## How verification is determined

Your account is treated as a verified seller when both are true:

- X account is linked
- Stripe account is connected and verified

## 1) Connect your X account

Open `/profile` and complete **Connect X account**.

## 2) Connect Stripe Seller Account

From `/profile`, start Stripe onboarding and complete all required details.

## 3) Refresh verification status

After finishing Stripe steps, return to `/profile` and use **Refresh** if status has not updated yet.

## What you unlock

- Verified seller badge on your profile/template presence
- Ability to publish paid templates and receive payouts
- Better buyer trust signals at listing level

## If status is still incomplete

- Re-open Stripe onboarding from profile and check missing requirements.
- Confirm X account remains linked.
- Retry status refresh after completing verification steps.
