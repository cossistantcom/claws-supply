title: Why Claws.supply Exists
description: Claws.supply helps builders ship OpenClaw setups faster with reusable, vetted templates.
---

Claws.supply exists to remove the "start-from-zero" tax for OpenClaw builders.

Instead of rebuilding the same foundations every time, you can start from templates that already capture practical structure, prompt patterns, and working defaults.

## Who this is for

- Builders launching OpenClaw agents quickly for real tasks.
- Teams who want repeatable starting points instead of one-off setups.
- Sellers who want to distribute useful templates and earn from their work.

## Why templates beat starting from scratch

- You move from idea to runnable setup faster.
- You reduce avoidable mistakes in early configuration.
- You can compare multiple approaches by category, recency, and popularity.
- You can keep improving over time by tracking template updates.

## The builder loop on Claws.supply

1. Discover templates and choose one that matches your use case.
2. Test safely in a controlled environment.
3. Customize for your own workflow.
4. Publish your own template when you find a useful pattern.
