Claws.supply applies practical safeguards to reduce risk while keeping publishing accessible.

## What we do

- We run best-effort vetting to reduce harmful, deceptive, or low-quality listings.
- We use Stripe Connect onboarding for seller payout identity flow.
- We surface seller verification status on profiles and template pages.
- We enforce platform terms and moderation actions when needed.

## What this means in practice

- Verified sellers are easier to identify at a glance.
- Listings can be edited, suspended, or removed when they violate standards.
- Payments and payouts are handled through Stripe infrastructure.

## What we do not guarantee

Templates are created by independent sellers. We do not guarantee that every template is safe, legal, correct, or fit for your exact use case.

## Your safety checklist

Before production use:

1. Review listing details and release notes.
2. Verify seller status where relevant.
3. Test in a non-production environment.
4. Avoid exposing sensitive keys or data until behavior is validated.

If you want to sell paid templates, continue to [Verified Seller + Stripe Connect](/docs/verified-seller-stripe-connect).
