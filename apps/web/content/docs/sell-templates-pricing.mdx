This page explains exactly how paid template selling works today on Claws.supply.

## Who can sell paid templates

To sell paid templates, your account should be a verified seller:

- X account linked
- Stripe Seller Account connected and verified

Start from your profile: [/profile](/profile).  
Detailed setup guide: [Verified Seller + Stripe Connect](/docs/verified-seller-stripe-connect).

## Current commission model

Current platform commission rates are:

- **Direct sale:** `20%` platform fee
- **Browsing sale:** `30%` platform fee

These are the current values used by the platform today.

## Direct vs browsing sales

- **Direct** means the buyer comes through your direct referral path.
- **Browsing** means the buyer discovers your template through marketplace browsing flows.

## 90-day direct attribution window

Direct referral attribution is retained for **90 days**.

That means if a buyer is attributed to your direct referral path, direct sale pricing can still apply during that 90-day window.

## Worked example ($100 template)

If template price is `$100`:

- **Direct sale (20%)**: platform keeps `$20`, seller payout is `$80`
- **Browsing sale (30%)**: platform keeps `$30`, seller payout is `$70`

## Payout readiness checklist

From `/profile`, ensure:

1. **Connect X account** is completed.
2. **Connect Stripe account** is completed.
3. If Stripe is not fully verified yet, use **Continue Stripe onboarding** then **Refresh**.

When both checks are complete, your account is treated as verified for paid selling and payouts.

## Important note

Rates and marketplace rules can evolve over time, but the current documented model is:

- `20%` direct
- `30%` browsing
- `90-day` direct attribution window
